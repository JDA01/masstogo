Bio

Marcos Escobar is a massage therapist, owner and CEO of Masso To-Go!

He studied in building design and then began his career as a technician in civil engineering.

Since he was very young, Marcos has always shown an interest in the field of sport (former goalkeeper, on ice, cosom and DEK). After 5 years of career in civil engineering, he decided to study at the AMS to learn the function and movements of muscle biomechanics. Graduate of the Academy of Scientific Massage: Massage Therapist Practitioner and Sports Massage (Swedish massage and sports).
